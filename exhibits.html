Our Exhibits

Discover the wonders of marine life through our immersive exhibits

Main Exhibits

Coral Reef Ecosystem

Experience the vibrant colors and diverse life of coral reefs. Our 500,000-gallon exhibit features over 100 species of fish and invertebrates.

  • Daily feeding shows at 11 AM and 3 PM
  • Interactive touch pool
  • Educational talks every hour

Deep Sea Exploration

Journey into the mysterious depths of the ocean. Our deep sea exhibit showcases bioluminescent creatures and deep-sea adaptations.

  • Special lighting effects
  • Rare deep-sea species
  • Research station viewing

Marine Mammal Habitat

Meet our resident seals and sea lions in their naturalistic habitat. Watch them play, swim, and interact with their trainers.

  • Training demonstrations
  • Underwater viewing
  • Educational programs

Special Exhibits

Jellyfish Gallery

Experience the mesmerizing beauty of various jellyfish species in our specially designed gallery.

Shark Encounter

Get up close with various shark species in our 750,000-gallon shark tank.

Kelp Forest

Walk through our three-story kelp forest exhibit and see how these underwater forests support marine life.
