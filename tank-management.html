Arduino Device Connection

Connect Arduino-based sensors and controllers for real-time monitoring of:

  • Water Quality Sensors: pH, temperature, salinity, dissolved oxygen
  • Environmental Controls: Lighting, filtration, heating/cooling systems
  • Safety Monitors: Water level, pressure, flow rate sensors
  • Automation Systems: Feeding schedules, water changes, emergency protocols

Device Management

Manage connected Arduino devices and configure monitoring parameters. Each device can be calibrated and programmed for specific tank requirements.

Emergency Protocols

Automated emergency response systems activate when parameters exceed safe limits:

  • Temperature Alerts: Automatic cooling system activation
  • pH Monitoring: Automated buffer dosing systems
  • Water Level: Overflow protection and backup pumps
  • Power Failures: Emergency generator activation

Data Logging

All sensor data is logged for analysis and regulatory compliance. Historical data helps optimize tank conditions and predict maintenance needs.
