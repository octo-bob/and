Our Conservation Programs

Coral Restoration

Our coral restoration program focuses on growing and transplanting coral fragments to damaged reef systems.

  • Active restoration sites in 5 locations
  • Over 10,000 corals planted annually
  • Community volunteer opportunities

Marine Mammal Rescue

We operate a 24/7 rescue and rehabilitation center for stranded marine mammals.

  • Emergency response team
  • Rehabilitation facilities
  • Release program

Ocean Cleanup Initiative

Regular beach cleanups and ocean debris removal programs.

  • Monthly beach cleanups
  • Plastic waste reduction programs
  • Educational workshops
